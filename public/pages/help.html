Help & Support

How to Join a Game

1. On the main page, enter the 6-digit PIN code provided by the game administrator

2. Tap "Prisijungti" to enter the game

3. Wait for the game to start and all players to join

4. Follow the on-screen instructions during gameplay

Game Rules

Bidding: Place bids on power plant outputs during the bidding window

Scoring: Earn points based on your bid accuracy and efficiency

Rounds: The game consists of multiple rounds across different time periods

Winner: Highest score at the end of all rounds wins

Troubleshooting

Invalid PIN? Check the PIN provided by the administrator. PINs are 6 digits.

Can't join? The game session may have ended. Ask the administrator for a new PIN.

Lost connection? Refresh the page to reconnect to your current game.
